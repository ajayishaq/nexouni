Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in their original condition with tags attached. Returns are free for orders over $50.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the US. Express shipping (1-2 days) and international shipping options are also available.

Do you offer size exchanges?

Yes, we offer free size exchanges within 30 days. Simply contact our customer service team to initiate an exchange.

Are your products ethically made?

Absolutely. We work with certified suppliers who adhere to fair labor practices and environmental standards.
